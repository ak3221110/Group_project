Must Visiting Destinations

Jalmandir

The Jal Mandir, or Water Temple, is a prominent Jain temple in Pawapuri, Bihar, India

The Great Buddha Statue

The Great Buddha statue (Daibutsu) is one of the popular stops on the Buddhist pilgrimage and tourist routes in Bodh Gaya, Bihar (India).

Kaimur Hill

The Kaimur Hills stretch from near Katangi in Madhya Pradesh to near Sasaram in Bihar.

Glass Bridge

The Rajgir Glass Bridge is a 200-foot-high, 85-foot-long, and 6-foot-wide glass skywalk in the Rajgir Forest of Bihar, India.

Bihar to host Khelo India Youth Games, Para Games in 2025

Introduction:

In an exciting development for sports in India, Bihar has been announced as the host state for the Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG) and Khelo India Para Games in 2025. This prestigious event marks a significant milestone for Bihar, highlighting its growing infrastructure and commitment to promoting sports and inclusivity.

Key highlights:

First Time for Bihar: This is the first time Bihar has been selected to host such a large-scale national sports event under the Khelo India initiative, launched by the Government of India in 2018 to revitalize the sports culture in the country.Event Scale: Thousands of athletes from across India will participate in the Youth Games, which showcase emerging talent in multiple sports disciplines. The Para Games will celebrate the abilities and achievements of differently-abled athletes, promoting inclusivity and diversity in sports.Venues and Facilities: Rajgir Sports Complex, the state's ultra-modern facility, will serve as a central venue for many events. Additional venues in cities like Patna, Gaya, and Bhagalpur are being upgraded to meet international standards. Anticipated Benefits:

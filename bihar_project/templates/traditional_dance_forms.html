Traditional Dance Forms from Bihar

Bhojpuri Folk Dance

Bhojpuri folk dance is an energetic and colorful dance form that originates from the Bhojpuri-speaking regions of Bihar. It is performed during festivals like Chhath and Holi, reflecting the culture and traditions of the local people.

Bidesia Dance

Bidesia is a traditional folk dance of Bihar that tells stories of separation and longing. It is commonly performed during folk theatre performances, and the dancers express deep emotions through music and dance.

Jat-Jatin Dance

The Jat-Jatin dance is a traditional dance form of Bihar performed by couples, often depicting the separation and reunion of lovers. The dance is energetic and symbolizes the joy of rural life and festivities.

Kajari Dance

Kajari is a traditional folk dance performed during the monsoon season, especially in the regions of Bihar. It is accompanied by songs that evoke the beauty of rain and nature, with movements that reflect the rhythm of rainstorms.

Maach Dance

Maach is a folk dance and drama performance from Bihar, which portrays various folk tales and stories. This traditional art form combines dance, music, and dialogue to narrate the cultural history of the region.
